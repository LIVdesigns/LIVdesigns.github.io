About Me

About the Artist


Olivia Guillermo is a graphic designer who grew up in Vallejo, California. While growing up, she has obtained a strong and eager passion for art and has developed the skills of painting and drawing in different mediums such as charcoal, pastels, acrylic and now a passion for digital art.

She is currently studying to be a Graphic Designer as a Digital Media Art Major at San Jose State University and plans on using the new skills that she has learned and have developed to become a future web designer.
